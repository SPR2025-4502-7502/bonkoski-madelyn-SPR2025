Things in my college house

Though we are only in college for four years, that is plenty of time to create endless memories and make friends that feel like family.

Whether it be a messy couch, a broken mailbox, a cluttered fridge, a pain chipped door or photos on a wall, all of these ordinary objects are reminders to me of the wonderful friendships I have made in college that feel like family.
